About Jateng

Get to Know About Jateng

Jateng (Jawa Tengah or Central Java) is a captivating region where cultural heritage, vast nature, and warm hospitality come together. From the awe-inspiring temples of Borobudur and Prambanan, to the serene highlands of Dieng Plateau and the bustling traditional markets of Solo and Semarang, Central Java offers a rich tapestry of experiences for every traveler. Whether you're exploring centuries-old traditions, savoring authentic Javanese cuisine, or embarking on adventures through volcanoes, beaches, and historic cities, Jateng promises memories that last a lifetime. With its unique blend of past and present, Central Java invites you to discover the soul of Indonesia.
