Visit Jateng

The Jogja trip by Santa Laurensia School was an unforgettable journey of culture, history, and connection. From iconic sites like Borobudur and Keraton Yogyakarta to hands-on experiences with batik and silver crafting, students explored the heart of Javanese heritage. More than a trip, it was a chance to grow, learn, and feel the rich blend of tradition and tourism that makes Jogja so special.
